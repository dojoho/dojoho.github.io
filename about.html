About Us

Membership

We have no auditions, and welcome new members of all ages, voices, and abilities. Just come along to a rehearsal, and see if you'd like to join in

Alternatively, email contact person to ask if you have any questions.

When and where we rehearse

We rehearse at Turner Sims, Southampton University, Wednesday Lunchtimes from 1.10 – 1.40 pm within university term time and there is a rehearsal on the afternoon on the day of each concert. Timetable link.

Costs

We have no membership fees, but do ask a voluntary contribution to producing the scores hired, typically £15.

Need to Read Music?

We have audio aids available online for our music, so you don’t have to be able to read music to sing with us.
